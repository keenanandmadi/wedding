Frequently Asked Questions

1. Plus Ones?

Yes, we encourage you to bring your partner if they aren't already on the invite list. Please just add their name when you RSVP by February 2027 so we have the correct numbers :)

2. Gifts?

Your presence is the present to us! But if you wish to send something, we have linked some charities we care about, or you can contribute to our honeymoon/home fund on our Gifts & Donations page.

3. Name changes?

We will both be keeping our names, no changes on either end :)

4. Sustainability & costs?

We know many of you are not Colorado-based, and this is obviously costly and has an environmental impact. We are committing to use as few single-use items as possible and will be trying to support all local businesses for the event. We want to make this as affordable as possible and are happy to connect folks who wish to carpool or share lodging (please respond "yes" to this in the RSVP form if you are interested).

5. Dietary restrictions?

Yes, please let us know in the RSVP form and we will accommodate. We will 100% have vegan and vegetarian options.

6. Bridal parties?

Nope, everyone is party people:)

7. What do I wear?

We will be dressed up! But we encourage you to wear whatever you feel best in. Don't feel pressure to buy something new, unless you want to .

8. Didn't you guys already get legally married?

Haha yes, we did! But now it is time for the big party!!!
