Getting There

We recognize that Durango is a bit remote, and travel can take some planning. If you have any questions about getting there, please don’t hesitate to reach out — we are happy to help!

✈️ Durango-La Plata County Airport (DRO)- Convenience at a Price

About 20 minutes from downtown Durango, this is the closest airport and the easiest option in terms of travel. However, flights here can be a bit more expensive.

flydurango.com

✈️ Albuquerque International Sunport (New Mexico)- (ABQ)

About a 4-hour drive from Durango. Flights here are often somewhere between Denver and Durango in price. But it is the the world’s only “Sunport,” and has very unique colour scheme as far as airports go

abqsunport.com
