Our entire wedding will take place in downtown Durango, Colorado.

Getting There

We recognize that Durango is a bit remote, so we have added some travel options if you are not based in Colorado. If you have any questions about getting there, please don’t hesitate to reach out:)

✈️ Durango-La Plata County Airport (DRO)

About 20 minutes from downtown Durango, this is the closest airport and the easiest option in terms of travel. However, flights here can be a bit more expensive.

flydurango.com

✈️ Albuquerque International Sunport (New Mexico) (ABQ)

About a 4-hour drive from Durango. Flights here are often somewhere between Denver and Durango in price. Also, it’s the world’s only “Sunport,” and has a very turqoise and drown aesthetitc

abqsunport.com

✈️ Denver International Airport (DEN)

Typically the cheapest option for flights, but it’s about a 6-hour drive to Durango. Efficient and utilitarian… with a surprising number of conspiracy theories...... flydenver.com
